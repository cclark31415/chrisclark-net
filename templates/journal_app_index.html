My Journal

This is the official home of the My Journal mobile application — a private, open journaling app for Android.

Features

  • Write and organize journal entries with titles and tags
  • Speak to text — dictate entries using your device's microphone
  • Completely private — entries stored locally on your device by default
  • Optional Google Drive sync — sync to your own Drive, no cloud accounts needed
  • No ads, no tracking, no analytics
